In this chapter, we talked about lots of theories, as encapsulation, object-oriented programming, event-driven programming, method, and framework. In detail, we discussed how the Hello World App works. A original method named ViewDidLoad() that is called every time a view is load. Last, the common process that a program is created. Here is one thing different, known as Package.

Encapsulation 封装

showMessage(sender:) 的实现对外界隐藏，button 不知道这个方法是如何工作的，只知道它要发送一条信息，这就是封装。

OOP will introduce in latter chapter.

event-driven programming: Objects listen for certain events to determine the flow of the app.

Method

每个对象提供特定功能和执行特殊任务，这些功能用表示成代码被称之为 method.

  1. Method must be declared in a class.
  2. func is a keyword to declare a method.
  3. @IBAction is used to expose the method to Interface Builder. 允许链接源代码到 Interface Builder 中的 UI 对象
  4. 参数(即输入)在括号中，这里 sender 是 UIButton 类型的参数。

Framework

UIKit, UIViewController, UIButton and UIAlertController come from the UIKit framework.

How to use?

import UIKit first

API: 内建函数 that make your life easier.

viewDidLoad()

当一个 view 被加载时，自动调用。我们可以在这个方法中改变 view 的状态，例如加入

view.backgroundColor = UIColor.black

使整个 view 的背景就变成黑色。

我们在 viewDidLoad() 前加入了关键词 override，因为这个方法本来是由 UIViewController 提供的，为了自定义，我们必须 「推翻」它。
